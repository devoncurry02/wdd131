Siteplan for Orchid & Éclait

Devon Curry - WDD131

Site Name

Orchid & Éclait

Orchid in the name makes the consumer think of freshness and natural beauty & Éclait in french means brilliance.



Site Purpose

This website provides information about this whipped tallow business. It allows users to learn about the business, the people behind the business, health benefits of tallow, and what products they can buy from them.



Scenarios

Where can I find tallow?
What makes tallow healthier than moisturizer and oil?
How can I contact the business?
What products do they offer?



Color Schema

The colors will be very simple, mostly white and gray as the pictures will bring in most color
